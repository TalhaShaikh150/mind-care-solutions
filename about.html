My Mission: Your Well-being

My mission is to bridge the science of medicine with the art of psychotherapy, offering a compassionate, trauma-informed space where healing and growth unfold through the integration of mind, body, and heart.

About Me

I am a Master Practitioner in Clinical Counselling and Psychotherapy with an international background as a Family Physician. With over 15+ years of combined experience in medicine and mental health, I specialize in trauma recovery, emotional regulation, and holistic healing.

Integrating Polyvagal Theory, CBT, Somatic Therapy, EMDR, and Trauma-Informed approaches, I support individuals, couples, and families in restoring safety, balance, and connection. My practice offers a compassionate and supportive space for healing, self-discovery, and meaningful growth.

My Philosophy

Healing is a deeply personal journey one that unfolds at its own pace. My role is to offer guidance, understanding, and support as you move toward greater clarity.

Safety

Emotional and physical safety are the foundation of all healing. I create a secure, supportive environment where clients can explore their experiences at their own pace.

Integration

Drawing on both medical knowledge and psychotherapy, I view wellness as the integration of mind, body, and emotion. This holistic approach promotes balance and long-term resilience.

Growth

Healing is not only about reducing distress, but about rediscovering strength, self compassion, and meaning. I support clients in growing beyond survival toward confidence, peace, and fulfillment.
