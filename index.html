Getting Started FAQ

Answers to help you begin your therapeutic journey with confidence.

How soon can I start therapy?

+

We typically schedule new clients within 3-5 business days after the initial assessment. We offer evening and weekend appointments to accommodate different schedules, and we prioritize urgent cases for quicker access to care.

What should I prepare for my first session?

+

No special preparation is needed. Just bring an open mind and be ready to discuss what brings you to therapy. You might find it helpful to think about your goals and what you hope to achieve through the therapeutic process.

Can I switch between online and in-person sessions?

+

Absolutely. We offer flexible scheduling that allows you to switch between online and in-person sessions based on your needs and preferences. Many clients appreciate the convenience of mixing both formats to maintain consistency in their care.

What is your cancellation policy?

+

We require 48 hours notice for cancellations to avoid charges. We understand emergencies happen and offer one grace cancellation per year. Late cancellations and no-shows are charged the full session fee to respect our therapists' time.
