Getting Started FAQ

Answers to help you begin your therapeutic journey with confidence.

What should I prepare for my first session?

+

No special preparation is needed. Just bring an open mind and be ready to discuss what brings you to therapy. You might find it helpful to think about your goals and what you hope to achieve through the therapeutic process.

What can I expect in my first counselling session?

+

The first session is a gentle introduction. We'll talk about what brings you to counselling, your goals, and any questions you may have. It's also an opportunity to see if we're a good fit to work together. My goal is to create a safe, supportive environment where you feel heard and respected.

How many sessions will I need?

+

Every person's healing journey is unique. Some clients find benefit after a few sessions, while others prefer ongoing support over several months. We'll discuss your goals and progress together and adjust the frequency as needed.

Do you offer online or virtual sessions?

+

Yes. I offer both in-person and secure online counselling sessions, allowing you to choose the format that feels most comfortable and accessible for you.

Do you work with couples and families?

+

Yes. I support individuals, couples, and families seeking to strengthen communication, rebuild trust, and navigate relational challenges in a safe, guided setting.

Is everything I share confidential?

+

Yes. Everything you share is strictly confidential, with a few legal exceptions (such as risk of harm to yourself or others, or when required by law).

What is your session fee?

+

Please contact me directly for current rates, as they may vary depending on session type (individual, couple, or family).

What are your accepted payment methods?

+

Payment can be made via e-transfer at the time of your session. Receipts are provided for all payments.

Do you accept insurance coverage?

+

Many insurance plans cover counselling services provided by a Registered Clinical Counsellor. Please check with your provider to confirm your eligibility for reimbursement.

What is your cancellation policy?

+

I kindly ask for at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. This allows me to offer the time to another client.

Do I need a referral to book an appointment?

+

No referral is needed. You can contact me directly to book a session or schedule a free consultation.
