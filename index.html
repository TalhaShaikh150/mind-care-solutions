Insurance & Billing FAQs

Do you accept my health insurance plan?

**Therapy.io is in-network with many major insurance providers** nationwide. During the sign-up process, you can easily verify your eligibility and co-pay amount by entering your insurance details. For out-of-network benefits, we provide super-bills you can submit for reimbursement.

How does the weekly billing work?

Our plans are billed every four weeks. This covers all services included in your chosen plan (unlimited messaging, group sessions, and 1-2 live sessions, depending on the tier). The price displayed is the *average weekly cost* for convenience.

Can I use my FSA/HSA account for payment?

Yes, mental health services are typically eligible for reimbursement through Flexible Spending Accounts (FSA) and Health Savings Accounts (HSA). You can use your FSA/HSA card directly during checkout.
