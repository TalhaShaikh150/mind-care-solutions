Motor Vehicle Accident Mental Health Recovery

Specialized psychological support for trauma, anxiety, and PTSD following motor vehicle accidents

Common Psychological Effects After MVA

  • Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)
  • Driving anxiety and phobias
  • Depression and mood changes
  • Sleep disturbances and nightmares
  • Relationship strain and isolation

Our MVA Recovery Approach

  • Trauma-focused cognitive behavioral therapy
  • EMDR for trauma processing
  • Anxiety management techniques
  • Gradual exposure for driving anxiety
  • Insurance documentation support
