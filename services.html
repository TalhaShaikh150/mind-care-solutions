Our Evidence-Based Therapeutic Approaches

Cognitive-Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

A highly effective, goal-oriented approach focusing on challenging and changing unhelpful cognitive distortions and behaviors. Excellent for **anxiety, depression, and stress management**.

Trauma-Informed Care (EMDR, etc.)

Specialized modalities like **EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing)** and trauma-focused CBT to process past events and reduce their emotional impact.

Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT)

Primarily used for **couples and family counseling**, EFT focuses on creating more secure emotional bonds and restructuring negative interaction patterns.

Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT)

Teaches essential life skills in **mindfulness, emotion regulation, distress tolerance, and interpersonal effectiveness**. Effective for managing intense emotions.
