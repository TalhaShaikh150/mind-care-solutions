Our Mission: Your Well-being

**Therapy.io** was founded on the belief that professional mental health care should be **accessible, affordable, and stigma-free** for everyone, everywhere.

A Global Network of Certified Care

Since launching in 2018, we have grown into a leading online mental health platform, serving over a million users. We connect you with a network of **30,000+ licensed therapists** across various specialties, ensuring you get the *right* help, not just the *available* help.

"We use advanced matching technology combined with strict professional vetting to maintain the **highest standards of clinical excellence** and ensure a strong therapeutic alliance from day one."

The Therapy.io Therapeutic Difference

Evidence-Based Practice

Our network prioritizes proven methods like **CBT, DBT, and EMDR**. We ensure you receive therapy rooted in clinical research.

Client-Centric Matching

We focus on the **therapeutic alliance**. Our proprietary algorithm matches you based on specialization, style, and demographic needs.

Outcomes Focused

We track progress using **validated clinical tools** to ensure your therapy is effective, helping you achieve measurable goals.

Commitment to Diversity & Inclusion

Mental wellness is deeply personal and cultural. We believe that **effective therapy requires a safe, inclusive, and affirming space.** Our network is purposefully built to be diverse, offering therapists who specialize in working with:

  • LGBTQIA+ Affirming Care
  • Cultural and Ethnic Identity
  • Religious and Spiritual Dynamics

Driven by a Personal Commitment to Care

"I founded Therapy.io after seeing firsthand the challenges people face in accessing consistent, quality mental health support. My goal was to remove the barriers—the commutes, the waitlists, the stigma—and create a space where healing is not just possible, but inevitable."
