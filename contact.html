Therapy Intake FAQ

How soon can I schedule my first therapy session?

We typically respond to intake forms within 24 hours and can often schedule your first therapy session within the same week, depending on availability.

Is the intake form secure for sharing personal information?

Yes, our intake forms are encrypted and HIPAA compliant. Your information remains confidential and secure, following Alberta's privacy regulations for mental health services.

Do you accept insurance for therapy sessions?

Many insurance plans cover counseling with a Registered Clinical Counsellor. We provide receipts that you can submit to your insurance provider for reimbursement.
